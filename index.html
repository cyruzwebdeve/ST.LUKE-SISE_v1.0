Our Mission

OUR HISTORY

Established in 1975, St. Luke Christian School and Learning Center has been preparing the children for the future for the last four decades. The school was established as a result of a vision to provide quality Christian primary education to the wider community of Camarines Norte and now expanding on surrounding suburbs.


The school officially started with approximately 20 students and has steadily grown in both size and influence since that time and has earned a reputation as a quality education provider within the wider community.


At St. Luke Christian School and Learning Center, we promote a vision to provide education which inspires character and leadership, and we strive to lead the students along a pathway of values-based experiences in their learning. This approach has been instrumental in creating a rich learning environment where values, character traits and leadership attributes are promoted amongst students and staff.


We are proud of our well-equipped classrooms, resources, grounds and advanced integrated curriculum across each grade levels from pre-school to elementary.


The school seeks to develop the whole child; physically, spiritually, emotionally academically and socially.Established in 1995, St. Luke Christian School & Learning Center has prepared children for the real future by focusing on holistic development.

Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 7:30 to 5:10 Monday to Friday. However, specific schedules may vary depending on grade level.

▶ How can I enroll my child?

Enrollment typically starts in April. You can visit our school office to fill out the necessary forms. Required documents include good moral, PSA (birth certificate), report card, Form 137, ECCD (For Preschool), 2pcs 2x2 picture.

▶ How can I stay updated on school events and announcements?

You can stay informed through our official communication channels, including school website and Facebook page. Important announcements are also sent via messenger and SMS.

▶ Does the school provide meals or a canteen service?

Yes, we have a canteen that offers a variety of healthy meal options. Students can bring their own lunch or purchase meals from the cafeteria. Weekly menus are posted in the school canteen.

▶ How does the school handle late pick-ups?

If a student is not picked up on time, they will remain under the supervision of their adviser or the school guards. Parents will be contacted immediately.

▶ What are the tuition and other fees?

Our tuition and other fees vary depending on the grade level. A detailed breakdown is available at the school office. Flexible payment plans may also be offered for your convenience.

▶ What is the school’s policy on mobile phones and electronic devices?

Students are not allowed to bring mobile phones unless permitted for educational purposes. Misuse of devices may result in confiscation, and parents will be informed.

▶ What should my child bring on the first day of school?

On the first day, students should bring their school supplies, including notebooks, pencils, and lunch. Specific requirements will be provided by the teacher.
